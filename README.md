# Consumer Goods Ad-Hoc Insights
## Project Overview

Atliq Hardware is one of India's leading computer hardware manufacturers. Management requested ad-hoc analysis to support data-driven decisions. Ten business questions were solved using SQL to generate meaningful insights.

# Business Requests
1. Markets where Atliq Exclusive operates in APAC

## Insight

Atliq Exclusive operates in eight APAC markets, with India being the most active market.

2. Unique product growth in 2021 vs 2020

## Insight

Unique products increased by 36.33%, indicating significant business growth.

3. Segment-wise unique products

## Insight

Notebook and Accessories segments have the highest number of products, while Networking has the lowest.

4. Segment with maximum increase in products

## Insight

Accessories recorded the highest increase in unique products.

5. Highest and lowest manufacturing cost products

## Insight

AQ HOME Allin1 Gen 2 had the highest manufacturing cost, while AQ Master Wired X1 MS had the lowest.

6. Top 5 customers with highest average discount

## Insight

Flipkart received the highest average pre-invoice discount in 2021.

7. Monthly gross sales analysis for Atliq Exclusive

## Insight

Sales declined during March-April 2020, likely due to COVID-19, and peaked in November 2020.

8. Quarter with highest sold quantity

## Insight

Quarter 1 recorded the highest total sold quantity.

9. Sales contribution by channel

## Insight

Retailer channels contributed the highest gross sales in FY2021.

10. Top 3 products by division

## Insight

N&S division recorded the highest sales volume.
